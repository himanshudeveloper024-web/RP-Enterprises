Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to CCTV, network and maintenance questions.

Get clarity on installations, AMC, system choice and support right from the homepage.

How much does CCTV installation cost?

Costs depend on the number of cameras, system type, and installation complexity. We provide a transparent quote after an initial assessment.

Which camera is best for my business?

We recommend IP cameras for most businesses due to better resolution, remote access, and scalability. The final choice is tailored to your site and budget.

Do you provide AMC services?

Yes. Our AMC packages include preventive maintenance, priority support, equipment checks and system updates.

Do you provide network setup?

We offer LAN, WAN, office networking and fiber optic installations for secure and high-performance data networks.

How long does installation take?

Project duration varies by scope. Most small to medium installations can be completed in days, while larger enterprise setups may take one to two weeks.
